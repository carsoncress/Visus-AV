The Technology

Why direct-view LED?

Not every display technology belongs in a luxury home. Direct-view LED was built for environments where image quality is non-negotiable.

Seamless at Any Size

No bezels, no seams, no visible tile lines. Direct-view LED panels tile invisibly from 80 inches to 30 feet and beyond — scaling to your wall, not the other way around.

Brilliant in Any Light

With peak brightness exceeding 1,000 nits, your display remains vivid and high-contrast even in a sun-drenched great room — no blackout shades required.

True Black, True Color

Each pixel is self-emissive, achieving genuine blacks and a wide color gamut that no projector or edge-lit panel can match — even at 4K HDR.

Built to Last

Commercial-grade LED modules are rated for 100,000+ hours of operation with no lamp replacements, no lens degradation, and no perceptible dimming over time.

How It Works

From first call to final frame.

A four-step process refined over years of high-end commercial integration — now applied with residential precision.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    We visit your space — or review architectural drawings if you're in early construction — to understand your vision, viewing habits, room conditions, and how the display will live within your interior design. No hard sell. Just clarity.

  2. 02

    Design

    We specify the right pixel pitch, aspect ratio, and cabinet configuration for your room. Our proposal includes detailed drawings, materials specs, and a transparent, itemized quote — coordinated with your builder, GC, or designer as needed.

  3. 03

    Installation

    Our crew arrives on schedule, works cleanly within your home, and coordinates directly with your trades. We handle structural mounting, cable routing, power management, and source integration — end to end.

  4. 04

    Calibration & Support

    Every Visus AV wall is professionally calibrated for color accuracy, brightness uniformity, and gamma before handoff. We train you and your household staff, then remain available for ongoing support and firmware updates.
